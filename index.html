Introduction

St George's Higher Secondary School Vennikulam, is located in central Kerala ,on the national high way 49,in a picturesque ,exquisite and serene ambience.

The school was upgraded as a higher secondary in 2014. St George's is wholly owned and managed by the St George Jacobite Syrian Cathedral karingachira.

Courses offered for XI and XII classes

Science stream combination: physics, chemistry, mathematics, biology
Commerce stream combination :accountancy, business studies, computer application, economics
Second languages offered :malayalam, hindi

Students are extremely well supported in our higher secondary classrooms through our teaching faculty. Our children are encouraged to be academically curious and seek deeper understanding beyond the facts and grades. They are also exposed to a huge range of learning experiences beyond the classroom and each individual is encouraged to develop their individual personal profile
